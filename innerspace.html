Emergency Medical Situations

If you see a friend in crisis (e.g., suicidal thoughts, severe distress), act immediately. Here's what to do in India:

Immediate Steps:
  • Stay calm and listen without judgment.
  • Call emergency services: 108 (Ambulance) or 100 (Police if danger).
  • Contact Tele MANAS (14416) for guidance.
  • If on campus, alert warden/security.
For Suicidal Crisis:
  • Remove access to harmful items.
  • Encourage professional help; do not leave alone.
  • Seek nearest hospital with psych unit (e.g., AIIMS, govt hospitals).
  • Awareness: Under National Suicide Prevention Strategy.
In life-threatening situations, call 108 immediately.
